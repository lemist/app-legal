NoteWhen Support

This page explains how to get help with NoteWhen and answers common questions about using the app.

Contact us

If you need assistance, report a bug, or request a feature, please contact us through GitHub Issues.

Common topics

Getting started

NoteWhen lets you define health events (such as symptoms, medications, sleep, or exercise) and record each occurrence with a timestamp and optional notes. Use the + button on the Events tab to add events, tap an event to view history, or use the widget for quick recording.

iCloud sync

Your data is stored on your device and synced through your private iCloud account when you are signed in to iCloud. If data does not appear on another device, confirm you are using the same Apple ID and that iCloud is enabled for NoteWhen in iOS Settings.

Apple Health

You can import workouts, sleep, and water from Apple Health, and optionally save entries you log in NoteWhen back to Apple Health. Manage read and write permissions in the Health app or iOS Settings. If access was denied, open an event detail screen and use the sync controls to request access again.

Reminders

Configure logging and medication reminders in Settings. Reminders are delivered as local notifications on your device only.

Widget

Add the NoteWhen widget to record events quickly from your Home Screen. The widget shows shortcuts for your active events. Event history is stored in the main app, not the widget.

AI monthly reports

Optional monthly reports summarize your tracked events using AI. Reports require an active subscription. Generated reports are saved in the app and synced via iCloud. AI output is for personal reference only, not medical advice.

Subscriptions

Manage AI monthly report subscriptions in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Purchases are processed by Apple; we do not store your payment card details.

Privacy

See our Privacy Policy for how NoteWhen handles your information.

Medical note

NoteWhen is for personal tracking only. It is not a medical device. Consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical decisions.
